Sharing© Water

Solo Project | Game Designer | 3 months

"Should people have equal access to water? If so, why is it being capitalized?" Sharing© Water is a satirical speculative design board game focused on consumer education in the style of Monopoly.

In-depth research of global water corruption case studies was conducted, from corrupt businesses to environmental impacts, and used as game elements.

For example, each country's water profit are ordered in terms of land profit on the board

Additionally, extensive prototyping and playtesting was conducted to adjust the rules and bring new ideas, especially in water's role as a currency

Liminal Floor

Solo Project | Game Designer, Software Developer ReactJS | 2 months


Liminal Floor is a study on achieving horror with minimal graphics through a multiplayer escape room experience.

What makes this project special is it's research, as I first studied the concept of Liminal Spaces by creating nine 64-voxel piece representations using Blender

I realized that connecting the pieces in a random order gave a new yet consistent experience. Which led to the extension of an escape room game created in ReactJS within two months.
