I Need to Escapee

Team: 5 | Role: Game Designer, Graphics | Timeline: 4 months

Escapee is a horror-comedy escape game where players need to urgently use the washroom and escape

Contributions

Planning

We all brainstormed what kind of game we wanted to create, narrowing it down to old-school horror rpgs such as “Ib”. We then brought it back to earth by connecting it to our university facility: the Innovation Wing. 

Game and Mechanic Designs

We then developed the theme into workable mechanics, such as timers, random events, collectibles, and ending triggers.

Pair Programming

As all of us were studying Unity together, we practiced pair programming to produce clean and efficient code

Graphics and Marketing materials

To create consistency and immersion, the two of us worked on providing original graphics and sprites for the entire game.
